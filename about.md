# About Citizens Initiative NZ

## Our Mission

Citizens Initiative NZ is dedicated to strengthening democracy in New Zealand by advocating for the implementation of binding citizens-initiated referendums.

## What Are Citizens-Initiated Referendums?

Citizens-initiated referendums are a form of direct democracy that allows citizens to propose legislation or constitutional amendments directly to the people for a vote. This empowers everyday New Zealanders to have a direct say in the laws and policies that affect their lives.

## Why It Matters

Currently, New Zealand has a system for citizens-initiated referendums, but the results are non-binding. This means that Parliament can choose to ignore the outcome of a referendum, even when there is overwhelming public support for change.

We believe that:
- **Democracy should be participatory**: Citizens should have the ability to directly influence policy decisions
- **The people's voice matters**: When a significant number of citizens support a measure, it deserves serious consideration
- **Accountability is essential**: Binding referendums create a check on parliamentary power

## Our Goals

1. **Raise Awareness**: Educate New Zealanders about the importance of binding citizens-initiated referendums
2. **Build Support**: Gather signatures and support for legislative change
3. **Create Change**: Work towards implementing a fair and effective system of binding citizens-initiated referendums
